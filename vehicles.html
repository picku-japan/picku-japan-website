Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you choose the right vehicle before booking your transfer or charter.

How do I know which vehicle is right for my group?

The best vehicle depends on passenger count, luggage size, route type, and whether your trip is an airport transfer, private transfer, or full-day charter.

Can luggage capacity change depending on the group?

Yes. Luggage capacity may vary depending on stroller use, sports equipment, oversized bags, and how many seats are in use.

Can I request help choosing a vehicle before booking?

Yes. If you are unsure, contact us with your passenger count, suitcase count, and route, and we can recommend the most suitable option.

Are these vehicles available for both transfers and charters?

Some vehicle types are more common for airport transfers, while others are better suited for sightseeing charters and larger group travel.
