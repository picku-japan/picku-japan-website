About Pick-U Japan

Safe, smooth, and easy private transfers in Japan

Pick-U Japan provides private airport transfers, city rides, and charter services for travelers who want a more reliable and comfortable way to move around Japan.

Flight Tracking

We monitor arriving flights into Japan so airport pickup timing can be adjusted if your flight is delayed.

Easy Communication

Our team can support you before and during the trip, and drivers can contact you directly when needed.

Comfortable Vehicles

Support You Can Reach

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers reduce messages and help customers book faster.

How do I meet the driver at the airport?

Your driver will meet you at the designated arrival area with pickup instructions provided in advance.

What if my flight is delayed?

Flight arrival times can be monitored and pickup timing adjusted accordingly.

Can I request a custom route or stop?

Yes. For charters and selected transfer services, custom requests can be arranged in advance.

How do I know which vehicle to choose?

Vehicle choice depends on passenger count, luggage size, and route. We can recommend the best fit.
