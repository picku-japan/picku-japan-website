Frequently asked questions

Clear answers help reduce booking hesitation and improve trust on the page.

Is this a shared ride?

No. This is a private transfer reserved only for your group.

Can you pick us up from Narita and drop us at our hotel in Tokyo?

Yes, this service is for direct transfer from Narita Airport to hotels or addresses in the Tokyo service area.

Do you provide meet and greet service?

Meet and greet with a name sign may be available as an optional add-on, depending on your product setup.

What information do I need to provide?

Please provide your arrival flight number, terminal if available, passenger count, luggage details, and your drop-off address or hotel name.

What if I have a lot of luggage?

Please declare the number and type of luggage before booking. Oversized or excessive luggage may require a larger vehicle.
