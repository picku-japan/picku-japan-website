Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before booking your Narita to Tokyo private transfer.

Is this transfer available in both directions?

Yes. This product covers transfer service between Narita Airport and Tokyo City. Please check the pickup details and route direction carefully during booking.

What if my flight is delayed?

For airport pickup services that include flight tracking, the arrival time can be checked against the landing flight in Japan. Please make sure your flight number is entered correctly.

Can I book this service for a hotel in central Tokyo?

Yes. This service is intended for Tokyo City area transfers. If your destination is outside the covered area, please select the appropriate transfer product instead.

Can I bring large luggage such as surfboards or golf bags?

Yes, but you should select the correct vehicle type and declare oversized luggage in advance so the vehicle capacity matches your group and baggage.

Will I receive confirmation before the service date?

Please review the booking conditions shown in the product widget carefully and contact support in advance if changes are needed.
