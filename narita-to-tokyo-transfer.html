Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before booking your Narita to Tokyo private transfer.

Is this transfer available in both directions?

Yes. This route is available between Narita Airport and Tokyo City. Please check the selected pickup and drop-off direction carefully during booking.

What if my flight is delayed?

For airport pickup services that include flight tracking, the arrival timing can be checked based on the landing flight in Japan. Please make sure your flight number is entered correctly.

Can I use this service for a central Tokyo hotel or apartment?

Yes. This service is intended for Tokyo City transfers. If your destination is outside the covered area, please book the more suitable transfer product instead.

Can I bring large luggage such as surfboards or golf bags?

Yes, but you should choose the right vehicle type and declare oversized luggage in advance so the vehicle can match both passenger count and baggage size.

Will I receive confirmation before the service date?

Please review the booking conditions shown in the product widget. If changes are needed, it is best to contact support as early as possible before the service date.
