A calmer way to move across Japan

Pick-U Japan focuses on private transportation that feels smooth, clear, and dependable. From airport pickup to intercity travel and private charter use, the goal is simple: make each trip feel easier from the beginning.

Good transfer service is not only about the vehicle. It is also about timing, coordination, luggage space, route clarity, and making travelers feel supported when they arrive in Japan.

That is why we focus on practical details that matter most, especially for families, small groups, travelers with luggage, and customers who prefer a more comfortable private travel experience.

Your transfer, step by step

A simple booking process designed for smooth travel in Japan.

01

Book your transfer

Choose your route, travel date, group size, and vehicle directly on our website. Complete your booking securely online in just a few steps.

02

Receive confirmation

After booking, you will receive a confirmation voucher by email. Our team will review your trip details to ensure everything is arranged correctly.

03

Trip reminder

About three days before your transfer, we will send a reminder email so you can confirm the pickup time, location, and travel details.

04

Meet your driver

On the day of your trip, your driver will contact you via WhatsApp before the pickup time to ensure a smooth and easy meeting.

Support remains available before and during your trip if needed.
