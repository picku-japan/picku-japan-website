Our Story

A transfer service built around what travelers actually need

Airport pickup should not feel confusing. Private transportation should not feel uncertain. Pick-U was created to offer a smoother and more reassuring travel experience in Japan, especially for airport arrivals, families, small groups, and travelers with luggage.

We understand that the most important parts of a transfer are often the simplest ones: being picked up on time, knowing where to meet the driver, having enough space for passengers and luggage, and receiving clear communication when plans change.

That is why we focus on practical service quality rather than unnecessary complexity. Our goal is to make each ride feel organized, comfortable, and easy from booking to arrival.
